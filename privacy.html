Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SpringLabs ("we," "us," "our") provides an app that analyzes video clips of athletic technique using AI and gives feedback on performance. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Video clips you upload. Used only to generate your technique analysis. We do not permanently store your video after analysis is complete.
  • Email address. Used to verify your subscription and identify your account.
  • Payment information. Collected and processed directly by our payment provider (Stripe, or Apple/Google for app store purchases). We never see or store your full card details ourselves.
  • Basic usage data (such as which features are used) to help us improve the app.

How we use your information

  • To generate AI-powered technique analysis from your uploaded clips
  • To verify an active subscription before granting access to analysis features
  • To communicate with you about your account or subscription
  • To improve and maintain the app

Third parties we work with

We use the following services to operate SpringLabs. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Anthropic — processes uploaded video frames to generate AI technique feedback.
  • Stripe — processes web-based subscription payments.
  • Apple / Google — process in-app subscription payments made through the App Store or Google Play.
  • Vercel — hosts our website and backend infrastructure.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data retention

Uploaded video clips are processed for analysis and not retained afterward. Your email and subscription status are retained for as long as your account is active, or as needed to comply with legal obligations.

Your choices

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through Stripe (web) or your Apple/Google account settings (app).
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us below.

Children's privacy

SpringLabs is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above.

Questions about this policy? Contact us at springlabs3@gmail.com

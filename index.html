      How it works

      Three lanes to a score.

      LANE 1

      Pick the event

      Sprint, hurdles, throws, jumps, or block starts — each has its own coaching checkpoints, so the AI knows what to look for.

      LANE 2

      Upload the clip

      A few seconds of video is enough. Side angle works best, but the model adapts to what you've got.

      LANE 3

      Get the breakdown

      A score out of 10, what's already solid, and the one or two fixes that will move the needle fastest.
